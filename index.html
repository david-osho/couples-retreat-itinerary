Precious Couples,

We are absolutely thrilled that you’ve confirmed your attendance for our Couples’ Retreat, themed "The Way We Were... Stronger Together." We can't wait to spend an incredible weekend with you from Friday, July 26th to Sunday, July 28th at the beautiful Macdonald Botley Park Hotel!

"Two are better than one, because they have a good return for their labour: If either of them falls down, one can help the other up." (Ecclesiastes 4:9-10)

Before we share what’s happening below, we would like you to Participate in our exciting quiz and stand a chance to win a prize!🎉 Also leave your feedback, testimonials, or anonymous questions in advance or during the retreat. Check out the quiz and the feedback sections below! 🏆

Friday, July 26th: Opening Our Hearts... Building Foundations

  • Welcome Reception: Kick off the weekend with a warm welcome and a fun meet-and-greet. Let’s get the good vibes rolling! 🍹
  • Ice-Breaker Activities: Enjoy light-hearted games designed to promote laughter and create an atmosphere of openness and camaraderie.
  • Movie Night: After dinner, snuggle up with your loved one and enjoy a romantic movie, sparking heartfelt conversations and deeper connections.

Saturday, July 27th: Unravelling Love's Mysteries… Deepening Connection

  • Rediscovery Workshop: Deepen your connection and communication with engaging activities and group reflections, followed by a delicious buffet lunch.
  • Afternoon Activity: Enjoy an intimate and fun activity to deepen your bond and enhance your knowledge of each other.
  • Photo Shoot: Dress up and capture beautiful moments of your love against the luxurious retreat backdrop.
  • Dance Night: After dinner, ignite the flames of passion as you dance to romantic tunes, creating moments of intimacy and joy.

Other things to do

  • Nature Walk: Absorb the beauty of creation by walking the hotel grounds. This tranquil setting is perfect for peaceful conversation and shared reflection.
  • Spa Retreat: Enjoy a refreshing spa to relax, rejuvenate, and rekindle the spark in your relationship with calming treatments. (Kindly note to book ahead at reception upon arrival)

Sunday, July 28th: Celebrating Commitment and Love’s Journey

  • Reflection & Vow Renewal: Start your day with a serene brunch, followed by a beautiful time of reflection and reaffirmation of your vows. Let's celebrate the love and commitment that has brought you here today. 💍❤️

Macdonald Botley Park Hotel, Winchester Road, Botley, Southampton SO32 2UA, United Kingdom

"Though one may be overpowered, two can defend themselves. A cord of three strands is not quickly broken." (Ecclesiastes 4:12)

This retreat is all about deepening your connection and making lasting memories together. We’re so excited to see you there and share in this special time.

Your hosts, Pastor & Mummy O
